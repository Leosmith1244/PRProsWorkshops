Recommended Books

Dive deeper into the theory and practice of public relations and crisis management with these foundational texts.

  • "Crisis Management: Planning for the Inevitable" by Steven Fink
    • A classic guide on preparing for, managing, and recovering from organizational crises.
  • "Contagious: Why Things Catch On" by Jonah Berger
    • Explores the science behind virality and how ideas spread, highly relevant for PR messaging.
  • "Trust Me, I'm Lying: Confessions of a Media Manipulator" by Ryan Holiday
    • Offers a provocative look at how modern media can be manipulated, crucial for understanding today's PR landscape.

Key Online Articles & Guides

Stay updated with the latest trends and best practices in crisis communication and public relations.

  • "The Anatomy of a Crisis: 8 Tips for Responding Effectively" (PRSA)
    • Practical steps for crisis response from a leading professional organization.
  • "How to Craft a Non-Apology Apology" (Harvard Business Review)
    • Strategies for expressing regret without admitting legal fault, a core concept in this workshop.
  • "The Edelman Trust Barometer" (Annual Report)
    • An essential global study on trust and credibility, invaluable for understanding public sentiment.

Useful Tools & Platforms

Leverage technology to monitor public sentiment, manage social media, and analyze your communication efforts.

  • Social Media Monitoring Tools (e.g., Brandwatch, Mention)
    • Track mentions, sentiment, and trends related to your brand or crisis.
  • Press Release Distribution Services (e.g., PR Newswire, Business Wire)
    • Efficiently disseminate your official statements and news.
  • Sentiment Analysis Software (e.g., IBM Watson Natural Language Understanding)
    • Analyze text for emotional tone and underlying attitudes to gauge public reaction.
