About the Workshop

Turn chaos into calm with 'Grace Under Fire'

December 2025 • Online via Zoom

In the unpredictable world of public relations, crises can emerge from anywhere. The Damage Control Dungeon workshop is specifically designed for Toastmasters members to master the art of crisis communication, transforming potential disasters into opportunities for triumph.

Using the robust framework of Toulmin’s Model of Argumentation, you will learn to construct bulletproof arguments that not only defend your reputation but also rebuild trust and win over skeptical audiences. This workshop will equip you with practical techniques to respond strategically and effectively.

What You'll Learn

  • How to spin a scandal into an ovation-worthy triumph
  • Crafting strategic non-apologies that defuse crises
  • Pivoting attention from negative narratives to positive actions
  • Winning competitive PR battles with logical arguments
  • Applying Toulmin's Model in real-world scenarios
